Simple description of a pet

A pet is an animal or a bird kept at home for plessure rather than the one kept for work or food. This is done World Wide at the moment. Different people have different taste as usual.

Here are some of the reasons why I love having a pet

  • Pets can help manage loneliness and depression by giving us companionship.
  • Some even offer security
  • Regular walking or playing with pets can decrease blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and triglyceride levels.
  • They can increase opportunities to exercise, get outside, and socialize.
